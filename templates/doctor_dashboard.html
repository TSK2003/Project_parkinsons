Patient onboarding

Create a new patient ID

The portal generates the patient ID from the first 4 letters of the patient's name and the last 4 digits of the mobile number. You choose the patient password here.

Example: Naveen Kumar with 9876543210 becomes patient ID nave3210. The patient will use that ID and the password you set here to log in.
Recording guidance

Capture cleaner screening audio

  • Use a quiet room and keep the phone close to the patient.
  • Record a steady 8 to 10 second sustained "Ahh".
  • The live waveform should move while the patient is speaking.
  • Retake any sample marked low confidence or retake recommended.
