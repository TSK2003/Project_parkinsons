Understanding Your Results
✅

Healthy Result

Green result means the model does not detect Parkinson's patterns in your voice. Healthy probability will be above 50%.

⚠️

Parkinson's Detected

Red result means the model detected vocal biomarkers associated with PD — jitter, shimmer, and HNR irregularities in your voice.

📊

Confidence Score

Higher % = more confident. If Parkinson's probability stays above 70% across multiple chunks, the signal is strong.

🔁

Multiple Chunks

One chunk = 2 seconds of voice. Wait for at least 5–10 chunks for a reliable prediction. Watch the history graph.

🎙

Best Practice

Say "Ahhhhh" in a quiet room at a steady pitch. Avoid background noise. Keep your mouth 15–20 cm from the mic.
